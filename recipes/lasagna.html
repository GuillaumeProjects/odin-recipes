Top best lasagna!

A simple recipe for all folks that love pasta, meat and cheese

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

Here are the typical ingredients to cook classic lasagna (Lasagna alla Bolognese):

For the meat sauce (Bolognese):

  • Ground beef (or a beef–pork mix)
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Tomato sauce
  • Herbs

For the béchamel sauce:

  • Butter
  • flour
  • Milk
  • Nutmeg
  • Salt
  • Pepper

For assembly:

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Mozzarella & parmesan

Step-by-step instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180 °C (350 °F).
  2. Sauté onions and garlic in olive oil.
  3. Add ground meat and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in tomatoes, herbs, salt, and pepper to make the sauce.
  5. Prepare a béchamel by mixing butter, flour, and milk.
  6. Butter a baking dish and lay down the first layer of lasagna sheets.
  7. Add a layer of meat sauce, then a layer of béchamel.
  8. Repeat the layering process to fill the dish.
  9. Finish with béchamel and grated cheese on top.
  10. Bake for 35–40 minutes until the top is golden and bubbly.
